About Aspire

Aspire is the flagship event of Enactus NSUT, bringing together visionary minds and industry leaders. Our business conclave features cutting-edge competitions in entrepreneurship, including case studies and business plan presentations.

Purpose

To foster innovation and entrepreneurship among students and young professionals.

Themes

Sustainable Development, Social Entrepreneurship, Tech Innovation

Activities

Case Study Competitions, Business Plan Presentations, Networking Sessions, Keynote Speeches

About Enactus NSUT

Enactus NSUT is a student-run organization at Netaji Subhas University of Technology, dedicated to creating a better world through entrepreneurial action. We develop and implement sustainable projects that address social, economic, and environmental challenges in our communities.

Project Kilkari

Empowering rural artisans through digital marketing and skill development.

Project Saksharta

Providing quality primary education to children and generating a source of income for the educated youth.
